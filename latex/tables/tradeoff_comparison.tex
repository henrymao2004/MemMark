\caption{\textbf{Where the $7\%$ utility cost buys you something
new.} Metadata provenance is free but forgeable once the
snapshot leaves the system. Token-level and action-layer
watermarks pay utility cost but cannot attribute in the
forensic snapshot-only setting (the output text / trajectory is
gone by the time the memory leaks). \memmark{} is the only point
in this matrix that succeeds at snapshot-only attribution, and
the price — comparable to text-layer watermarks — is the
controlled trade-off this paper offers.}
